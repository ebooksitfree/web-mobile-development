Book Description
-----------------


    
    Conquer the world of Android app development&nbsp;
Android has taken over the mobile and TV markets and become unstoppable! Android&nbsp;offers a vast&nbsp;stage for developers to&nbsp;serve&nbsp;millions—and rake in the profits—with&nbsp;diverse and wide-ranging&nbsp;app ideas. Whether you’re a&nbsp;raw&nbsp;recruit or a veteran programmer,&nbsp;you can get in on the action&nbsp;and become a master of the Android programming universe&nbsp;with&nbsp;the&nbsp;new&nbsp;edition of&nbsp;Android&nbsp;Application Development&nbsp;For&nbsp;Dummies All-in-One.&nbsp;In addition to&nbsp;receiving guidance on&nbsp;mobile and TV development, you’ll&nbsp;find&nbsp;overviews of native code, watch, car, Android wear, and other device development.&nbsp;
This&nbsp;friendly, easy-to-follow&nbsp;book kicks&nbsp;off&nbsp;by&nbsp;offering&nbsp;a&nbsp;fundamental understanding of Android’s major technical ideas,&nbsp;including&nbsp;functional programming techniques. It&nbsp;moves&nbsp;on to&nbsp;show you how to work&nbsp;effectively in Studio,&nbsp;program cool new features,&nbsp;and&nbsp;test&nbsp;your app to&nbsp;make sure&nbsp;it’s&nbsp;ready to&nbsp;release&nbsp;to&nbsp;a&nbsp;waiting&nbsp;world. You’ll also&nbsp;have an opportunity to brush up on your&nbsp;Kotlin&nbsp;and develop your marketing savvy. There are millions of potential customers out there,&nbsp;and you want to stand out&nbsp;from the crowd!&nbsp;&nbsp;
Understand&nbsp;new&nbsp;features and enhancements&nbsp;Get development best-practices&nbsp;Know your&nbsp;Android hardware&nbsp;Access online materials&nbsp;With a market share like Android’s, the stakes couldn’t be higher.&nbsp;Android&nbsp;Application Development&nbsp;For&nbsp;Dummies All-in-One&nbsp;levels the field and gives&nbsp;you the tools you need&nbsp;to take on&nbsp;the world.&nbsp;&nbsp;
